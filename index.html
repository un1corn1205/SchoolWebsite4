School Achievements


Robotics Competition

DAIS students bring home 2nd place in the National Robotex Competition

Four of our Class 3 students, which formed Team Robotechies, comprising of Aarav Nair, Aarush Bharti, Avan Mehta and Purnaansh Patel, won second prize at the National Robotex Lego Line Following Competition held in Ahmedabad. This was a two-tier competition with a qualifying regional championship conducted in Indore, Mumbai, Delhi, Rajkot and Ludhiana. 'Team Robotechies' qualified in the Mumbai regionals by securing second position. This was followed by their feat at the National Tournament amidst 18 teams from 8+ age category. The team built a robot using Lego Ev3 blocks, that won the Line Following challenge with high precision and high speed.

Lego Leauge Junior Winners

Our Juniors Win The Lego League Competition

Ved Ramchand and Arnav Shah of Class 1 B were part of a team that competed at the First Lego League Junior Competition, comprising of about 50 other teams. The task was to build a Model City called “Boomtown Build”. Their Team was awarded the “Most Co-operative Model” award.

MSSA Winners

DAIS Wins The Prestigious MSSA Trophy

DAIS has won the Niranjan Ganjawala Trophy for the Best Coeducational School in Sports in Mumbai,for the year 2019 - 2020. The trophy is presented by the Mumbai School Sports Association (MSSA) to the coeducational school which has secured the maximum points in the year in all the sporting competitions held, across all the sporting disciplines and age groups. DAIS has won the prestigious trophy for the third time. We had also won the trophy for the years 2009 - 2010 and 2014 - 2015.
